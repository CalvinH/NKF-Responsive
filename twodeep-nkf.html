Our Mission The National Kidney Foundation, a major voluntary nonprofit health organization, is dedicated to preventing kidney and urinary tract diseases, improving the health and well-being of individuals and families affected by kidney disease and increasing the availability of all organs for transplantation.

Our Vision To enhance the lives of everyone with, at risk of or affected by kidney disease. View Overview Video →
